# Privacy Policy for Aviation Weather Discord Bot
### Last Updated: May 5th, 2026

Your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how the Aviation Weather Discord app collects and handles user information.

## Information We Collect

### Discord User ID
We store your Discord User ID to remember your privacy policy agreement, including the version you agreed to, the date you agreed, and your data collection preferences. This allows us to respect your choices across sessions without requiring you to re-agree each time.

### Anonymous Usage Statistics
We may collect anonymous usage statistics to help us understand how the bot is used and improve the product over time. This data contains no personally identifiable information — your Discord User ID is never included in or linked to these statistics. You may opt out of anonymous usage statistics at any time using `/privacy-settings`.

## Information We Do Not Collect
- Your name, email address, phone number, or any other contact details
- Message content or command arguments beyond what is necessary to fulfill a request
- Any data from your Discord account beyond your User ID

## Real-Time Data and Permissions
To provide accurate aviation weather (such as METARs, TAFs, PIREPs, AIRMETs, and SIGMETs), the app requires an internet connection to fetch real-time weather data from official sources (e.g., NOAA, Aviation Weather Center). These requests are made on your behalf and are not linked to your identity.

## Third-Party Services
Our app fetches data from public aviation weather APIs. While we do not send your personal information to these services, your device's IP address may be visible to these providers during a standard data request, similar to browsing a website. We do not control how these third-party providers handle technical logs, though they are typically used for server maintenance and security.

## Your Rights
- **Opt out** of anonymous usage statistics at any time via `/privacy-settings`
- **Delete** all data we hold about you (your User ID and agreement record) at any time via `/privacy-settings`

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date. Significant changes may require you to re-agree before continuing to use the bot.
