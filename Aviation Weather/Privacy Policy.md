# Privacy Policy for Aviation Weather Discord Bot
### Last Updated: January 3rd 2026

Your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how the Aviation Weather Discord app handles user information.

## Information Collection and Use
We believe that your personal data belongs to you. Aviation Weather does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).
- No Personal Data: We do not ask for or collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other contact details.
- No Tracking: We do not use third-party trackers, analytics, or advertising identifiers to monitor your behavior across other apps or websites.
- No Account Required: You are required to have a Discord account to interact with Aviation Weather, but are not required to provide any credentials.

## Real-Time Data and Permissions
To provide accurate aviation weather (such as METARs, TAFs, and SIGMETs), the app may require specific device permissions:
- Internet Access: The app requires an internet connection to fetch real-time weather data from official sources (e.g., NOAA, Aviation Weather Center). These requests are anonymous and made on your behalf.

## Third Party Services
Our app fetches data from public aviation weather APIs. While we do not send your personal information to these services, your device’s IP address may be visible to these providers during a standard data request, similar to browsing a website. We do not control how these third-party providers handle technical logs, though they are typically used for server maintenance and security.

## Data Security
Because we do not collect any user data, there is no database of your personal information on our servers to secure or breach.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
